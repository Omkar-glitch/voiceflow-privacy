VoiceFlow Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 9, 2024

Summary: VoiceFlow processes your voice locally and through secure AI services. We don't store your voice recordings or transcriptions. Your API keys are encrypted on your device.

Information We Collect

VoiceFlow collects minimal information necessary for the app to function:

  • Voice recordings: Temporarily captured when you tap the microphone button, processed immediately, then deleted.
  • API keys: Your Groq and Gemini API keys are stored encrypted on your device only.

How We Use Your Information

  • Voice-to-text conversion: Your voice is sent to Groq's Whisper API for transcription.
  • Text formatting (optional): Transcribed text may be sent to Google Gemini for formatting.

We do not use your data for any other purpose.

Third-Party Services

VoiceFlow uses these third-party services:

  • Groq API: For speech-to-text conversion. See Groq's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Gemini API: For optional text formatting. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Security

  • API keys are stored using Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences
  • All network communication uses HTTPS encryption
  • Voice recordings are never saved to storage
  • No data is sent to our servers â€” we don't have any

Data Retention

VoiceFlow does not retain any of your data. Voice recordings are processed in memory and immediately discarded. Transcribed text is inserted into your app and not stored by VoiceFlow.

Children's Privacy

VoiceFlow is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store personal data, there is no data to access, correct, or delete. You can uninstall the app at any time to remove all locally stored preferences.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Significant changes will be communicated through app updates.
